The Case for ScoopTrack

Two Leaks. One Fix.

⚠️

The Problem

Ice cream shops bleed margin from two directions at once — and most owners can't see either clearly.

1

Overproduction waste

Slow flavors get batched too often, sit unsold, and get scrapped — pure preventable loss.

2

Popular-flavor stock-outs

Running dry on bestsellers on busy days drives customers away and kills revenue.

3

Gut-feel planning

Most owners rely on memory and paper logs. The patterns exist in the data — no one's capturing them.

✓

The Solution

ScoopTrack turns daily shop data into production decisions you can trust.

→

Real-time inventory levels per flavor with automatic low-stock alerts

→

Logged daily production batches that build a data trail over time

→

Seasonal trend analysis that spots bestsellers before the rush hits

→

Data-driven production recommendations — what to make and how much
