## What it is
A web application purpose-built for independent ice cream shop owners that brings flavor inventory tracking, daily production batch management, and seasonal demand trend analysis together in one focused operations tool.

---

## The Problem
Ice cream shops bleed margin from two directions simultaneously:

1. **Overproduction waste** — slow-moving flavors get batched too often, sit unsold, and get scrapped. Pure, preventable loss.
2. **Popular-flavor stock-outs** — running dry on bestsellers on busy days drives customers away and kills same-day revenue.
3. **Gut-feel planning** — most shop owners rely on memory and paper logs. Demand patterns exist in the data, but no one is capturing or acting on them.

Neither problem is random. Both are predictable with the right data and the right tool.

---

## The Solution
ScoopTrack gives ice cream shop owners a smart operations hub:

- **Flavor Inventory Tracker** — real-time stock levels per flavor, depletion rate visibility, and automatic low-stock alerts before you run out on a busy day.
- **Daily Production Batch Log** — log each batch as it's made (flavor, quantity, timestamp). Build a production history that improves planning week over week.
- **Seasonal Trend Analysis** — demand patterns by flavor, day of week, and season. Know which flavors peak in summer vs. winter before the rush hits.
- **Smart Production Recommendations** — data-driven batch suggestions based on the shop's own trends: what to make today and how much, so owners waste less and sell more.

---

## Primary Users
- **Ice cream shop owners** (primary decision-maker and daily user)
- **Shop managers** (daily batch logging and inventory updates)

Target segment: small-to-medium independent ice cream shops.

---

## Core Goal
Always have the right flavors ready. Waste less. Sell more.

---

## Positioning
A practical, business-first operations tool for hands-on shop owners who want data without complexity. Not a POS system, not a full ERP — a focused inventory and production intelligence layer that maps directly to the rhythms of a working shop.

---

## Tone & Voice
Calm, confident, and practical. Numbers and actionable signals over charts for their own sake. No jargon. Built for owners, not developers.

---

## Key Business Outcomes
- Reduce flavor waste (cut over-batching of slow-moving flavors)
- Eliminate popular-flavor stock-outs (keep bestsellers on hand every day)
- Replace gut-feel planning with production history and trend data
- Spot rising seasonal flavors before the rush so supply can meet demand
